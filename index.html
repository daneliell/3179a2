Change in Gold Prices From 2000-2020

Gold has always been an important factor in world economics. Examining the prices of gold across different countries is a good estimate of their economic status.

Throughout the years, the price of gold of countries worldwide have been steadily rising. The chart above shows the percentage change in price of gold of ten different currencies from 2000 to 2020.

Click on the legend to filter out values. Multi-selecting compares the values of different currencies.

If you want to isolate a period of time in the chart, select the area you wish to view in the bottom graph.

Gold Reserves by Country

A gold reserve is the gold held by a national central bank. It is important to support the value of national currency.

Similar to the price of gold, the size of gold reserves worldwide have been rising. The map above shows the value of each country's gold reserve valued in billions USD.

Drag the slider to view values from specific years from 2000 up to 2018.

You can hover over each country to view their precise value.
